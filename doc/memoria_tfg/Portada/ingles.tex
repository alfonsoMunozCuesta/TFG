\section*{Expansion and Improvement of an Interactive Dashboard for Survival Analysis Applied to University Dropout}

\noindent
Muñoz Cuesta, Alfonso(2026). Trabajo de Fin de Grado. Córdoba: Universidad de Córdoba.
\section*{Abstract}
This Final Degree Project aims to extend and improve an interactive web application focused on survival analysis applied to university dropout. The proposed solution allows users to load and preprocess academic data, apply statistical techniques such as Kaplan-Meier, Cox regression, the Log-Rank test, Exponential and Weibull models, and incorporate Random Survival Forest to analyse risk profiles. In addition, the system provides interactive visualisations, comparison between techniques, multilingual support, export of results into PDF reports, and automatic interpretation through local artificial intelligence, making it easier to study the factors associated with dropout and to understand the results obtained.

\section*{Keywords}
Survival analysis; university dropout; interactive dashboard; statistical models; Random Survival Forest; local artificial intelligence.
